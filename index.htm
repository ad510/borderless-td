One of the major reasons I chose to make this game over other games is that I have been fascinated with infinitely large randomly generated worlds, yet I have never written code to generate one myself. Therefore, while this demo page does not implement a complete game, it is a proof of concept that shows I can generate an infinitely large forest. Click on the ground to move, and click the yellow thing in the corner to build a wood cutter. Then explore the forest that never ends!

I recommend playing this in Google Chrome because it seems to lag in Firefox if you play it for too long.
